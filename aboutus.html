About Us

Here at Ricco, we have your best interest at heart. You're hungry? We have food. You need a stress-free environment? Our decor is clutter free, seats by windows if you prefer that, seats in the back if light is not your thing. We play soft classical music in the background, at a decent volume so you can talk to people without yelling, and it'll be comfortable if you prefer to eat in silence. We promise you only the very best when you step into our restaurant. We want it to be worth your money as much as you want it to be worth your money.
